Avengers

Tony Stark

Alias: Iron Man

Tony Stark is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor. He is the former CEO of Stark Industries and the chief source of funding and equipment for the Avengers. Using his brilliant mind, he created the Iron Man suit to protect the world as a superhero.

Steve Rogers

Alias: Captain America

Steve Rogers was a frail young man who was enhanced to the peak of human physicality by an experimental serum to aid the United States government's efforts in World War II. He became Captain America and the leader of the Avengers.

Natasha Romanoff

Alias: Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff is a highly trained spy and former agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. She is one of the most talented assassins in the world and fights for the Avengers as Black Widow.

Bruce Banner

Alias: The Hulk

Bruce Banner is a brilliant scientist who transforms into a green-skinned behemoth with incredible strength whenever he becomes angry or agitated. As the Hulk, he brings tremendous power to the Avengers.

Thor

Alias: God of Thunder

Thor is the Norse God of Thunder, the son of Odin, and the prince of Asgard. He wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, which gives him the ability to fly and control weather. As a founding member of the Avengers, he defends both Earth and Asgard.
